# Inventory Supply Chain Management Analysis Dashboard
1. 📌 Project Summary : 
Developed an interactive Inventory Supply Chain Management Dashboard in Power BI to monitor inventory performance, warehouse efficiency, order fulfillment, transportation costs, and supply chain operations.
Designed the dashboard to help businesses optimize inventory levels, reduce stock shortages, improve warehouse utilization, and enhance supply chain decision-making.
Enabled real-time monitoring of key inventory and logistics KPIs through dynamic visualizations and interactive filters.

2. 🎯 Business Objectives : 
Monitor inventory performance across regions and product categories.
Analyze warehouse utilization and storage efficiency.
Track inventory turnover and stock movement.
Evaluate transportation costs across regions.
Identify backorders and fulfillment bottlenecks.
Optimize supply chain operations and inventory planning.

3. 🔍 Key Insights : 
Warehouse utilization stands at 34.08%, indicating available storage capacity for future inventory growth.
Inventory turnover ratio of 23.47 reflects efficient inventory movement and stock management.
Average inventory holding period is 15.56 days, suggesting fast inventory circulation.
Units sold increased significantly from 2022 onward, reaching nearly 200K units by 2024.
Transportation costs vary across regions, with certain categories incurring higher logistics expenses.
Electronics and Clothing categories contribute significantly to transportation costs.
Fulfilled orders represent the majority of order volume, indicating strong operational efficiency.
Backorders are concentrated within pending orders, highlighting opportunities to improve replenishment planning.
Inventory levels vary across regions and categories, supporting region-specific inventory strategies.

4. 🏆 Business Recommendations : 
Improve warehouse utilization through optimized inventory allocation and storage planning.
Reduce pending orders by enhancing demand forecasting and replenishment processes.
Focus on minimizing transportation costs in high-expense regions.
Maintain optimal inventory levels to prevent stockouts and excess inventory.
Strengthen inventory planning for high-demand product categories.
Continuously monitor inventory turnover to improve working capital efficiency.
